About Me

Since I was young, I have always been inspired by the world around me. In 2010, video editing and digital design became a major way I expressed my love for the things I was passionate about. Looking at my past work, I see that I'm ultimately driven by my unique perspective on relating to the world around me. No matter the medium, I enjoy inspiring creativity and passion in others.


It is my passion to design, edit, and create. In 2018 I decided to further pursue my passion for coding at Southern Methodist University. Given my extensive design background, I have a niche in front end and UI/UX development but over the years I have obtained skills that make me a well rounded full stack developer. I am eager to learn and am excited to see what is next on my journey.

In my free time you'll find me on the ice playing hockey or running with my dogs in the mountains near our home in Breckenridge, Colorado. I am a big animal lover and have a passion for caring for animals. I am currently interested in finding a way I can utilize my techincal and design skills in a field pertaining to bettering the care and lives of animals. It is important to me that I am passionate about the work I do at the next company I decide to work for!

Feel free to reach out by email or connect with me on LinkedIn!
